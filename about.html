About BIOCCE

Who We Are

Founded in Shanghai, BIOCCE is a professional floor scrubber manufacturer and industrial cleaning equipment supplier serving clients in over 50 countries. We specialize in walk-behind floor scrubbers, ride-on floor scrubbers, industrial sweepers, material handling equipment, lifting equipment, garden machinery, aerial work platforms, and other industrial products.

As a factory-direct supplier, we offer competitive pricing without compromising on quality. Every product is manufactured under strict quality control standards and carries CE, ISO, and other international certifications.

Our team brings decades of combined experience across multiple industrial equipment categories. From product selection to after-sales support, we work closely with each client to ensure the right equipment for their specific operational needs.

As a floor scrubber manufacturer based in China, we offer factory-direct pricing on both walk-behind floor scrubbers and ride-on floor scrubbers. We welcome global buyers seeking a reliable industrial cleaning equipment supplier in Shanghai with competitive FOB pricing, CE certification, and professional export documentation.
