Why Floor Scrubbers Are Essential for Logistics Centers in Central Asia

Central Asia is experiencing a rapid expansion in logistics and warehousing infrastructure. New distribution centers are opening across Almaty, Nur-Sultan, Tashkent, and Shymkent, driven by the growth of e-commerce and cross-border trade along the Belt and Road corridors. With this expansion comes the need for professional floor cleaning equipment that can maintain large warehouse spaces efficiently.

The Growing Logistics Sector in Central Asia

Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan are emerging as key logistics hubs connecting China, Europe, and the Middle East. Modern logistics centers in these countries now rival international standards in size and complexity. Facilities of 10,000 to 50,000 square meters are becoming common, and traditional manual cleaning methods simply cannot keep pace.

Why Traditional Cleaning Falls Short

Using mops and buckets in a 20,000 m² distribution center is not just slow — it is ineffective. Manual cleaning leaves floors wet for extended periods, creates slip hazards for forklifts and workers, and fails to remove embedded dirt and tire marks that accumulate in high-traffic zones. A single spill that is not cleaned immediately can disrupt operations and create safety risks.

The Floor Scrubber Solution

Industrial floor scrubbers offer a fundamentally better approach to warehouse cleaning:

Speed and Productivity
A ride-on scrubber like the BIOCCE BC1250 cleans up to 6,600 m² per hour — approximately 10-15 times faster than manual methods. A 20,000 m² logistics center can be fully cleaned in a single 3-4 hour shift, without disrupting daytime operations.

Safety First
Modern scrubbers combine scrubbing and vacuum drying in one pass. The floor is left bone-dry within seconds, eliminating slip hazards. Built-in safety features include automatic shutoff, reverse alarms, and LED lighting for visibility.

Consistent Quality
Unlike manual cleaning, which varies from worker to worker, scrubbers deliver consistent results every pass. This is especially important for logistics centers serving international brands that audit facility cleanliness.

Cost Efficiency
While the initial investment in a floor scrubber may seem significant, the return on investment is typically 6-12 months. Replacing 3-4 manual cleaners with one machine and one operator saves labor costs while improving cleaning quality.

Choosing the Right Machine for Your Logistics Center

For logistics centers in Central Asia, we recommend considering these factors:

  • Facility size — Up to 10,000 m²: walk-behind or compact ride-on (BC600). Over 10,000 m²: mid to large ride-on (BC1000 or BC1250)
  • Aisle width — Narrow aisles require compact machines with tight turning radius
  • Operating hours — Multi-shift facilities benefit from lithium battery scrubbers that can opportunity-charge during breaks
  • Floor type — Epoxy, sealed concrete, or tile floors may require different brush types and pressure settings
