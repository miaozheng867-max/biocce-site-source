Electric Pallet Truck

Brand: BIOCCE | Model: PT-20
Model: PT-20

The BIOCCE PT-20 is a reliable electric pallet truck designed for efficient material handling in warehouses, distribution centers, and manufacturing facilities.

With a 2000kg load capacity and lithium battery, it delivers consistent performance across long shifts with fast charging capability.

Key Features

  • Ergonomic tiller arm with integrated controls for operator comfort
  • Lithium battery — no maintenance, fast charge in 2 hours
  • Smart walkie/rider mode with speed limiting for safety
  • AC drive motor for smooth acceleration and precise control
  • Emergency reverse button and auto brake on tiller release

Applications

Warehouses, logistics centers, retail distribution, cross-docking facilities, manufacturing floors.
